Step 1

Download and install a wallet that supports Solana, such as Phantom or Solflare. After installation, create a new wallet and securely back up your recovery phrase.

Step 2

Acquire Solana (SOL) tokens from a cryptocurrency exchange like Binance, Coinbase, or Kraken. Transfer the purchased SOL to your newly created wallet address.

Step 3

Navigate to Raydium's Swap Page and click on "Connect Wallet." Select your wallet provider (e.g., Phantom or Solflare) and authorize the connection.

Step 4

On the Raydium Swap interface, select SOL in the "From" field and paste the CA in the "To" field. Enter the amount of SOL you wish to swap and review the transaction details, including the estimated amount of $NUBU you'll receive. Click "Swap" and approve the transaction in your wallet.
